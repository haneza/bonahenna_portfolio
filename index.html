About me

I'm Boglárka Nagy tattoo artist, graphic designer, henna painter...anything is creative.:)

Bonahenna was created in 2016, when I was working with henna painting, and soon after I fell into the world of tattoos. I made my first tattoo on my father's leg when I was 16 years old. With that, the learning process began - which I think like in many other fields lasts for the rest of our lives.

In 2019 I started working as a junior tattoo artist in a salon in Budapest. Following in 2021 I collaborated with other artists and we opened our small salon: the Bloomingmind Tattoo shop.
‍
I completed my graphic studies at the Buda School of Drawing in 2021, and since then I have been working as a graphic designer and freelance artist.
